Play Full Screen

iPhone Safari can't hide its address bar. To play full-screen, add Rescue James to your Home Screen:

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari (square with up arrow).
  2. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  3. Launch from the new icon on your Home Screen.
